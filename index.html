Privacy Policy - Local Ticket Saver

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Overview

Local Ticket Saver helps you store and view your travel ticket files on your device.

2. Data Collection

Local Ticket Saver does not collect, store, or share your personal data on external servers. Your ticket details and selected files are stored locally on your device.

3. Permissions

The app may request file access permission so you can choose and open ticket files. This permission is used only for app functionality.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Local Ticket Saver does not sell or share your data with third parties.

5. Data Security

Since data is stored locally, keeping your device secure helps protect your information.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the contact method provided on the app's Play Store listing.
